Magic Cake

Magic cake is one of the testiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as a result of consuming Magic Cake is your responsibilty. You've been warned, and you can view our legal page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. lit sit at room temperature for 20 mins
  5. Eat and enjoy
